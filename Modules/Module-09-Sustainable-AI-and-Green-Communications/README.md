# Module 9: Sustainable AI and Green Communications

## Overview

Module 9 introduced the importance of sustainability in modern telecommunications and explored how Artificial Intelligence can improve energy efficiency while reducing the environmental impact of communication networks. Through the course materials and research articles, I learned how AI helps optimize network operations, reduce energy consumption, and support environmentally responsible communication technologies. This module emphasized the growing importance of balancing technological advancement with sustainable practices.
