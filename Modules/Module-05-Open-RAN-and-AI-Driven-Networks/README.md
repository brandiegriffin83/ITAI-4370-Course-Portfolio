# Module 5: Open RAN (ORAN) and AI-Driven Networks

## Overview

Module 5 introduced Open Radio Access Networks (Open RAN) and how Artificial Intelligence is transforming wireless communication networks. Through the course materials and research articles, I learned how Open RAN creates a more flexible and interoperable network architecture while AI improves automation, resource management, and network performance. This module demonstrated how open standards and intelligent technologies are shaping the future of 5G and beyond.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, I was able to:

- Describe how Artificial Intelligence supports Open RAN.

- Explain vendor interoperability.
- Analyze emerging research related to Open RAN technologies.
